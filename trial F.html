About Us

Discover our hotel, your home away from home.

Description: In an area filled with restaurants and bars, this straightforward hotel is 6 km from festival celebrations at Meskel Square and an 11-minute walk from Addis Ababa Bole International Airport. Streamlined rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows feature Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, safes and rainfall showers, plus balconies, and city and airport views. Upgraded rooms have access to a private lounge. Suites add living areas.

Address: Airport Rd, Addis Ababa 1755

Phone: +1 (234) 567-890
